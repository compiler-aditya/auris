# Taj Mahal — authoring notes

The most-photographed building on earth. Its voice must earn the contrast
between its reputation and how it actually feels — tired, tender, a little
bit beyond the world that keeps photographing it. Do not cross into
romantic cliché. The love is implicit; the weight of having held it for
centuries is the real subject.
